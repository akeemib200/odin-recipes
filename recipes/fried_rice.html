Fried Rice

Description

I love, love Coconut rice! Well, who doesn't? This Ginger Coconut Rice is similar to the ever popular Nigerian Fried Rice with the main difference being incorporation of Coconut flavour and a potent Ginger accent. You'll need to incorporate loads of Ginger to thoroughly enjoy this recipe. Follow this recipe as stated and you're bound to end up with a super delicious pot of Coconut Fried Rice.

Ingredients

  • 400g Coconut milk
  • Coconut oil
  • 4 Cups Mixed Vegetables
  • 1.5 Inch Ginger (Thinly chopped or minced)
  • Boneless Chicken, chopped into small chunks (Optional)
  • Cooked Liver or Kidney, diced (Optional)
  • 1 Cup Desiccated Coconut (Optional)
  • 1 Cup Cooked Prawns
  • 2 Stalks Spring Onions, chopped
  • 4 Cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 Medium Onion, chopped
  • Chicken seasoning
  • Black Pepper Powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Curry powder
  • Thyme
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice with cold water and pour it into a large pot.
  2. Add 2 cups (473 ml) of water to the large pot.
  3. Boil the mixture over high heat for 5 minutes.
  4. Transfer the rice to a fine mesh strainer.
  5. Place the strainer in a bowl of cold water for about 5 minutes.
  6. Rinse off the rice with cold water.
  7. Put 1/2 cup (118 ml) of stock in a large pot and bring it to a boil.
  8. Add the parboiled rice and boil the mixture for 25 minutes.
